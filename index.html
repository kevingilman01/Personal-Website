About Me

Education

I am a current student at the University of Arizona. I am pursuing a master's degree in electrical and computer engineering, as well as a minor in data science and statistics. Through my coursework, I have developed an understanding of both hardware and software. The projects that I have completed have helped me to better understand the full engineering design process.

Work

I work for an HVAC company called EDGE Mechanical. My current role at the company is controls engineering. This involves both programming and wiring/installation. As a controls engineer, it is my responsibility to configure controllers to drive different HVAC equipment. After the controllers are programmed, I wire the necessary external devices to the input and output ports of the controller. Once the controller is wired, I perform an air balance by testing the equipment, and monitoring values in real time to make final adjustments in the software.
